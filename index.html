Abstract

Making timely orders and queuing up for cash payments for goods and services in Madhanvi Industrial Park at is difficult. The different goods and services are distributed all-over this park into different ware houses, each with it's sales department at ware-house level but all under central management of Madhanv industry. This means a client has to move from one ware house to another to physically make orders & payments for goods & services they need. This traditional system of ware house management is so stressful and time-consuming for customers.

Brief

Welcome to Madhvani Warehouse management App.

The Warehouse management app team has developed an application that looks to solve the mentioned problem through a mobile application that alows users to make orders online, have them processed and then receive their products through delivery.

Madhvani Group In Uganda

The Group’s assets in Uganda are valued in excess of USD 200 million, with several units undergoing major rehabilitation / renovation / expansion programmes. Madhvani Group the forefront of industrial development in several countries and is one of the largest diversified private-sector groups in East Africa, employing over 10,000 people. The application offers security through use of Authentication via email and or phone number. Then they are led to the home page which features a variety of products that are organised in the following categories:

  • Trending

Item search

Item selection

Item Ordering

The user can search for any existing product of his/her choice using the search feature.
Upon the return of results, the user can then select the product and the quantity that he/she desires. The users can also select different products and then add them to 'cart'.
After the user is done making the choice(s), they proceed to the purchase confirmation

In the purchase confirmation, they will be required to input their pin to verify purchase
